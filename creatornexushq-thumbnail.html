💡 Thumbnail Best Practices
FACES CONVERT
Thumbnails with expressive human faces get 30-40% higher CTR. Eye contact with the camera works best.
3 SECOND RULE
Your thumbnail must communicate the video's value in 3 seconds at mobile size. If it can't pass that test, redesign it.
LESS IS MORE
Max 3-4 words of text on a thumbnail. Anything more gets ignored. Let the image do the heavy lifting.
CONTRAST WINS
High contrast between your subject and background makes thumbnails pop in the feed. Avoid busy or dark backgrounds.
COLOR PSYCHOLOGY
Red and yellow trigger urgency. Blue builds trust. Green suggests success. Use 2-3 dominant colors max — avoid rainbow thumbnails.
TEST AT SMALL SIZE
70% of YouTube views are on mobile. Shrink your thumbnail to 120x68px before publishing — if it's unclear at that size, fix it.
