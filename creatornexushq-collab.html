💡 How to Land a Collab

1️⃣
Watch their content firstReference a specific video in your outreach. Generic messages get ignored — specific ones get responses.
2️⃣
Lead with valueDon't ask what they can do for you — tell them what you bring to the table and why it benefits them.
3️⃣
Start smallA shoutout swap or 30-minute co-stream is easier to say yes to than a full collab video. Start small, build the relationship.
4️⃣
DM beats emailFor smaller creators, sliding into Twitter/X or Instagram DMs gets a faster response than a formal email.
5️⃣
Follow up onceIf they don't respond in a week, one polite follow up is fine. After that, move on and try someone else.
